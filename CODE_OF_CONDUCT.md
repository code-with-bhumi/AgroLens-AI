# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone contributing to the **AgroLens-AI** system.

## Expected Behavior
All participants are expected to:
* Be respectful and considerate in all technical and project-related communication.
* Demonstrate empathy and professionalism at all times.
* Share constructive feedback on code reviews and collaborate in good faith to reach project milestones.
* Be inclusive of all backgrounds, perspectives, and levels of technical expertise.
* Honor project ownership and accurately credit specific contributions to data cleaning, EDA, and preprocessing.

## Unacceptable Behavior
Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:
* Harassment, bullying, or abusive language in repository comments or group chats.
* Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, nationality, disability, or any protected characteristic.
* Personal attacks, insults, or technical elitism.
* Deliberate disruption of the four-week engineering roadmap or version control protocols.
* Uploading inappropriate or offensive images, code, or datasets that violate professional standards.

## Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness behavior that compromises our professional environment, please report it immediately:
* **Contact:** Use the primary project communication channel agreed by the team.
* **Escalation:** If the team is unable to resolve an issue internally, it should be escalated to our project mentor or organization sponser.

## Enforcement
As the Team Lead, I will work with the group to address conduct violations, which may include:
* Asking individuals to revise or remove inappropriate commit messages or documentation.
* Temporarily restricting access to specific repository branches until an issue is resolved.
* Removing contributions or Jupyter Notebooks that violate project security or privacy standards.

The ultimate goal is to maintain a positive, enterprise-grade environment where our group can collaborate effectively to deliver a high-impact AI portfolio piece.

## Attribution
This code of conduct is adapted from industry-standard open-source community guidelines to fit the specific needs of the AgroLens-AI research and development team.
